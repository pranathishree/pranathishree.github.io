Problem Statement
The core growth challenge I identified in Scorptech
Great product.
Invisible
to the world.

From my analysis, Scorptech's biggest challenge isn't what they build — it's who knows they build it. They have the skills, the values, and the work ethic. What they lack is a front door. In a market full of noise, a great studio without a distribution strategy stays hidden — no matter how good they are.

P1
Their brand awareness is near zero — potential clients can't find them without a direct referral.
P2
They lack trust signals — without a visible portfolio or testimonials, converting visitors is very difficult.
P3
Their distribution is weak — no active channel is pushing qualified leads toward them.
P4
They haven't tapped the creator economy — influencers wanting to go digital are a perfect fit, yet unreached.

06
Solutions & Prioritisation
My recommended strategy for Scorptech's growth
01
Target Businesses with Existing Customers
In my view, their best early opportunity is reaching out directly to restaurants, local businesses, and service providers who already have a strong customer base and great products — but no digital presence. Helping them go online increases sales and user experience, and gives Scorptech a clear, tangible pitch.
High Priority
02
YouTube & Web Advertising
They should run targeted YouTube pre-roll ads showcasing their builds, and retarget website visitors via Google Display. Focusing on intent keywords like "build business website" and "app for my restaurant" will bring the right audience to them.
High Priority
03
Client Referral Programme
Once they have happy clients, they should build a structured referral loop — both parties earn a discount when a referral converts. Featuring testimonials and case studies prominently will do a lot of the selling for them. One delighted business owner can organically bring three more.
High Priority
04
Influencer & Creator Partnerships
They should partner with mid-tier creators (10K to 200K followers) who are building personal brands or online businesses. A co-build or affiliate deal — where Scorptech builds their site in exchange for a dedicated post or video — gives them credible, authentic reach.
High Priority
05
SEO Content & Community Presence
They should publish case studies and guides, and engage actively on ProductHunt, IndieHackers, and Reddit. Offering free website audits is a smart way to demonstrate their value and build reputation organically over time.
Medium Priority
