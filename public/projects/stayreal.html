A product strategy for how stay-booking platforms — hotels, villas, rooms, and homes — can close the trust gap and deliver experiences that actually match what users were promised.

About this project — This is an industry-wide product strategy, not specific to one company. It covers platforms like Airbnb, Booking.com, MakeMyTrip, OYO, and similar apps and websites where users discover and book stays. The goal is to identify the deepest user pain points across these platforms and define solutions that any of them could adopt to meaningfully improve user experience.

02
Problem Statement

Travelers book based on photos and promises — but far too often, reality doesn't match. Trust is broken at every stage of the journey, across every major platform.

Fake Listings & Misleading Photos
  • Luxurious photos hide dirty or tiny rooms
  • Wide-angle edits create false expectations
  • "Sea-view villa" has a distant window crack
  • Advertised amenities missing on arrival
  • Fake reviews inflate property trust scores
Scam & Fraud Risks
  • Fake payment links and phishing emails
  • Fraudulent WhatsApp booking messages
  • Fake host accounts and duplicate listings
  • Hackers exploiting booking and messaging systems
  • Fake customer support numbers online
Property Doesn't Match Description
  • "Private pool" is actually shared
  • "WiFi available" but completely unusable
  • "Breakfast included" not provided
  • "AC room" with broken air conditioning
  • "Parking" available but severely limited
Poor Customer Support
  • No quick human agent when needed
  • Endless chatbot loops, no resolution
  • Support unavailable during emergencies
  • Users stranded with no help late at night
  • No ownership of dispute resolution

Solutions
01

Real Verification System

AI-powered listing verification that compares uploaded photos against reality. Hosts must submit verified media before a listing goes live on any platform.

  • 360° video walkthroughs with timestamps
  • AI image comparison — flags mismatches instantly
  • Verified Host badge on passing listings
  • Warning badge + ranking penalty on mismatches
  • Reality Match Score™ shown on every property

Travel Rescue Support

24/7 emergency human support built into every stay platform. One tap connects to a real agent — no bots, no loops, no waiting when it matters most.

  • One-tap SOS emergency call inside the app
  • AI handles routine queries, humans handle crises
  • Fast relocation and refund assistance
  • Hotel dispute resolution within hours
  • Available even offline via SMS fallback

Safety-First Certification

Every property earns a neighborhood safety score. Special certifications for solo travelers and women — making underserved travelers feel genuinely secure before they book.

  • Women-Safe Property certification badge
  • Verified neighborhood safety score
  • User-rated: lighting, entry security, staff behavior
  • Solo-travel friendliness rating
  • Hidden camera policy enforcement and reporting

Reality Match Score™

A unique trust metric shown on every listing. Each property earns a score based on user-uploaded media, complaint history, and amenity accuracy — visible before booking.

  • Score built from real guest photos and reviews
  • Amenity accuracy and complaint tracking
  • Cleanliness consistency tracked over time
  • Low-score properties flagged automatically
  • Hosts incentivized to maintain high scores
