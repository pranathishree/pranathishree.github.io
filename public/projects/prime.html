02
Problem Statement

Prime Video leads on value but lags on experience — users are quietly migrating to smoother platforms.

  • Intrusive mid-stream ads despite paying for a full subscription
  • No social or co-watching features — streaming is now a shared experience
  • Slower personalization innovation compared to Netflix and Disney+
  • Lower premium perception weakens brand loyalty and accelerates churn

04
Solutions
01

Smart Ad Placement

Replace disruptive mid-stream ads with a single, short ad placed only at the very start or end of a title. Users retain the feeling of uninterrupted viewing while Amazon preserves ad revenue.

  • One non-skippable ad — at start or end only
  • Zero mid-stream interruptions
  • Ad-free feel without losing revenue
  • Premium subscribers feel respected

02

PrimeSync — Watch Together

Reintroduce co-watching as PrimeSync. Friends sync playback in real time, send emoji reactions, and chat live. Designed for Gen Z's social-first digital habits.

  • Synced playback for 2–5 friends
  • Live reactions and chat panel
  • No video call needed — low infrastructure cost
  • Launch on mobile + desktop first
  • Increases session length and app retention
